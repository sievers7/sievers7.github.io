My Future

My plans for my career are pretty straightfoward and mostly revolve around the medical field. There are two areas of the medical field I want an MD in: Trauma Surgery, and Holistic Medicine.

Trauma Surgery:the branch of surgical medicine that deals with treating injuries caused by an impact. For example, a trauma surgeon may be called to the emergency room to evaluate a patient who is a victim of a car crash.

It is something that to me seems fufilling and although taxing, I would enjoy. In comparison to other medical fields is one one with the most variation. On top of being exciting and operating on unorthodox cases, the training for Trauma Surgery is more well rounnded and would undoubtably benefit my education more than say neurological surgery or cardiovascular surgery.

Holistic medicine:a whole-body approach to healthcare. It aims to improve health and wellness through the body, mind, and soul. Usually, holistic medicine combines traditional medicine and complementary and alternative medicine.

My mom went through nine years of medical schooling, and in the end she opened up her own holistic practice and became a pretty renound doctor incorporating traditional and western medicine. That's for the most part what I want to do. After about ten years of operating as a Trauma Surgery, it's highly likely that I'm going to be mentally exhaused and I can simply create my own Holistic practice and use my education in all aspects of medicine.
