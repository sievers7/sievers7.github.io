Motocross

My all time favorite activity during warm(ish) months is without a doubt Motocross. I haven't bee ndoing it for too long, but I love the adremeline rush and leaning curve that comes with it. My uncle started teaching me almost two years ago since he used ride A class. Although I'm nowhere near that level of skill, I enjoy every second on my dirtbike. The picture below is similar to the MX 508, which is a dirtbike track me and my uncle go to whenever we have free time while it's warm out. In addition to MX 508, we also go to MX 23 both of which are in Massachutes, but well worth the drive. If we ever want to just go for a calm senic ride through the woods we just head up to the Thomaston Dam, which is actually pretty close to Taft.

Wrestling

I've done various styles of martial arts, starting when I was five, but this year I decided to try something different; I started wrestling at Taft. The season didn't go that great for me, but I had a lot of fun and learned a lot that I hope to take with me next year. I put it last on my hobbies simply because I haven't been doing it as long as my other hobbies, but it is certaintly somthing I with to continue.
